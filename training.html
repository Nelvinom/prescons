The aim of this course is to provide learners with necessary practical skills required to ensure safe ascent to and descent from a height. This training entails communicating the responsibilities related to working at heights which include fall prevention and fall protection.

This course is designed to empower participants with the right tools to raise awareness on mental health and reduce stigma associated with mental illness.
